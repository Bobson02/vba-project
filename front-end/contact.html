Frequently Asked Questions

What sets EduCravings apart from other learning platforms?
EduCravings is dedicated to serving underserved communities specifically in rural and war-torn areas of Africa. Our platform is designed to address the unique challenges faced by these communities, such as limited access to quality education resources and connectivity issues. We prioritize inclusivity, accessibility, and resilience in our educational technology solutions.

How does EduCravings plan to bridge the digital divide?
EduCravings aims to provide equal access to educational resources and tech solutions, ensuring quality education for all students, regardless of their background. Furthermore, it allows students to download and access educational materials on when internet is not strong or not stable.

What support do users of the platform gain after completing their courses?
EduCravings platform offers support for top performers after courses, connecting them with internships and job placements, promoting professional growth and development beyond their educational journey.
